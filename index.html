About PriceWaiter

PriceWaiter exists to make free markets freer -- starting with a simple but ingenious platform that makes negotiation fast, simple and private, anywhere. Our culture abounds with trust, teamwork, high standards and a sense of exploration. Backed by a syndicate of leaders in e-commerce, conversion, and high-growth ventures, we offer the benefits you’d expect from a healthy startup, as well as stock options for team members who show initiative, resourcefulness, loyalty, and a little bit of true grit. PriceWaiter is growing fast but there’s still time to get in on the ground floor.
